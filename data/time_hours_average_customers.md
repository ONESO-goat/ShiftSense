## Get, Push, Go

The average time to push the carts to the store then back out is around **40** seconds to **1 minute and 25 seconds**, this also depends on the amount the pusher has, the distance, and the strangh of the pusher. The average amount of carts a pusher pushes is **4**.


- 1-4 carts + mid-sections = 30s
- 5-7 carts + mid-sections = 35s

- 1-4 carts + far-end-sections = 35s
- 5-7 carts + far-end-sections = 45s-55s (cars, mistakes, etc)

## Summary

For an effective and quick cart department, **2.5** pushers along the **entire day** for each entrance provides highest result while being least cost effective for the store.
